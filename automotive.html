NEXAR AUTOMOTIVE

Nexar provides visual-based and Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication-based ADAS software to automotive manufacturers and suppliers that can be ported into vehicle resident and infotainment systems as well as aftermarket devices.

Nexar’s Vehicle Network based on V2V communication over cellular networks is growing rapidly and already includes 120,000 drivers worldwide. We are currently working with several automotive manufacturers and suppliers, and encourage the rest to join our Vehicle Network and benefit from the many services it provides, including:

  • Over-the-top, on-demand sensor data collection services using vehicle sensors
  • Extensive datasets of labeled road imagery and unique long-tail incident data to train autonomous systems in responsiveness and situation characterization
  • Joint ADAS applications and driving-perceptron algorithm development
