SUMMARY

  • Nexar aims to make the roads safer for drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists by creating the world's largest open vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) network and providing real-time alerts to prevent collisions. The Nexar software uses smartphones’ and vehicles’ cameras and sensors to create driving perception and ADAS alerts and well as documented evidence in case of a collision.

  • Applying deep learning and artificial intelligence technologies over millions of crowdsourced road miles, Nexar provides a connected fleet safety solution, and a series of data products and services for the Automotive, Insurance, and Ridesharing industries.

  • Founded in 2015, Nexar has offices in Tel Aviv and San Francisco, and is backed by Aleph, Mosaic Ventures, True Ventures, Tusk Ventures and Slow Ventures. It is a member of the Berkeley DeepDrive consortium together with other leading members of the automotive industry. For more visit: https://www.getnexar.com/

conception

Nexar was founded by Eran Shir and Bruno Fernandez-Ruiz in early 2015.
Eran Shir Co-founder & CEO

Is a serial entrepreneur and investor, currently trying to change the driving world for the better. Previously, Eran was Entrepreneur In Residence at Aleph.VC. Before Aleph, Eran was an executive at Yahoo! following Yahoo!’s 2010 acquisition of Dapper, a startup he co-founded in 2007.

Bruno Fernandez-Ruiz Co-founder & CTO

Is a technologist on a mission to change the world using data and machine learning at large scale. Before Nexar, Bruno was a Yahoo! Senior Fellow, leading ad targeting and native advertising teams, and chief architect for cloud and platform. Prior to that, Bruno held technology leadership roles at Fidelity Investments and Accenture’s Center for Strategic Research Group.
