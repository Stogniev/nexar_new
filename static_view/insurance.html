Bringing Vision Context To Telematics

Enabling every driver a safer ride and more affordable auto insurance

NEXAR TELEMATICS A new era of mobility is bringing together safety, personalized insurance products, and a connected-claims experiences. Nexar is leading the charge in applying machine vision and AI to better understand driving behaviour, protect drivers and guide safer driving habits.
Vision based telematics Headway distance and relative-to-traffic speed are two of the first pure vision-based driving scores developed to better assess and develop Pay How You Drive (PHYD) products and better profile driver behavior.
Adding context to Telematics Measuring “Black box” car maneuvers as braking, acceleration, cornering and speeding is done in context of the driving scene in order to reach better precision in driving profiling.
Preventing the Coming Accident Nexar ADAS capabilities are driven by machine vision as well as vehicle-to-vehicle safety message communication. Keeping drive safe and focused on the is our first priority.
Collision Reconstruction Report In the unfortunate event of an accident, Nexar can provide an easy to read reconstruction report of the accident at millisecond resolution including analysis all telemetry on top of GPS and video footage.
Providing drivers with feedback Driver’s can view all their driving events, play their videos and learn how to improve. The closed loop of driving feedback assure driver can adopt better driving habit and raise awareness to risky driving habits.
