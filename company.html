Our Mission
A World Without Car Crashes
We make driver assitance software to protect you and your business against road collisions and casualities. We do it by using computer vision AI and inter-vehicle networking (V2V). We believe that AI-driven road safety is a nessecity rather than a luxury and should be in the hands of every driver, rich or poor. Join us on our journey to make the road safer, for everyone, starting today.
